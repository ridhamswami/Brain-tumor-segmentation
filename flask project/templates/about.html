About Glioma Detection System

Gliomas are a type of brain tumor that can significantly impact patient outcomes. Early detection and accurate diagnosis are critical for determining appropriate treatment strategies and improving survival rates. However, analyzing gliomas in MRI scans is often challenging, as it requires precision and expertise. Manual segmentation is time-consuming and prone to errors, which can delay treatment and affect patient prognosis.

Our Glioma Detection System leverages cutting-edge artificial intelligence and machine learning to automate the process of glioma segmentation and analysis. Using advanced 3D U-Net models, we can accurately segment gliomas from MRI scans, providing radiologists and oncologists with precise data in a fraction of the time required by manual methods.

Additionally, our system uses radiomic features extracted from the segmented tumors to predict patient survival, offering insights that assist in personalized treatment planning. We also differentiate between pseudoprogression and true tumor recurrence—two conditions that often look similar but require very different treatment approaches.

The solution aims to streamline the diagnostic process, enhance clinical decision-making, and ultimately improve patient outcomes. By integrating this system with an easy-to-use web interface, healthcare professionals can upload MRI scans, visualize segmented tumor regions, and receive accurate survival predictions, all from a single platform.
